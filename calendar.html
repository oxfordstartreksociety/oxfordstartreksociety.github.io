Where and when?

We meet every week during term at 7.45pm on Mondays. The location can vary.

On our Discord Server, we have a channel called #monday-nights for questions.

The location is usually at Jesus College (pinned on the map) but some meetings are held online via Discord.

Feel free to email us for the latest info, or follow our Facebook page.

Joining the mailing list

Send an email to us or fill out this poll and we'll add you. We send out emails every week with information about the time and place of the meeting and the theme for the episodes we'll be watching.
