Where and when?

We meet every week during term at 7.45pm on Mondays. The location can vary, check out our Groupspaces page for the latest email from the Captain with details on the time and place, or join the Facebook group. It's usually at Univ (pinned on the map).

Joining the mailing list

Send an email to the Captain and they'll add you. We send out emails every week with information about the time and place of the meeting and the theme for the episodes we'll be watching.
