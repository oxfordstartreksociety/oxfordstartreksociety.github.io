Where and when?

We meet every week during term at 7.45pm on Mondays. The location can vary, email us for the latest info, or join the Facebook group or Discord Server. It's usually at Jesus College (pinned on the map) but some meetings are held online via Discord, contact us if you would like to join our server.

Joining the mailing list

Send an email to us and we'll add you. We send out emails every week with information about the time and place of the meeting and the theme for the episodes we'll be watching.
